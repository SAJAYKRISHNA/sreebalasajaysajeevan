Kunjithai Temple
Sree bala subrahmanya swami kshethram Kunjithai

Kunjithai Sree bala subrahmanya Swamy Temple , popularly known as the Kunjithai Kshethram , is located in the small town of North Paravoor in Kunjithai village of Ernakulam district. Majority of the people depend on fishing for their livelihood. The temple is situated on the banks of Periyar river. Kunjithai Kshethram is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Palani Murugan. This temple is under "Sree Chithira Vaala Samajam".
